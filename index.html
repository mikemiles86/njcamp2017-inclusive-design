Inclusive Design vs. Accessibility

Inclusive Design

Accessibility


Universal design describes a set of considerations made to ensure that a product, service, and/or environment is usable by everyone, to the greatest extent possible, without the need for adaptation or specialized design. Accessibility is only slightlydifferent, describing the degree to which a product, service, and/or environment is made available to everyone.
