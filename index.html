The average user does not exist

Building for the "average user" is the same as building for no user. Every user has a unique difference

Create personas with limitations

  • "Wears glasses to correct for farsightedness"
  • "Has a broken right wrist due to a skiing accident"
  • "Does most browsing on phone while in the bathroom"

Plan beyond the average user

Planning for unique users (outliers), means capturing everyone in between.
